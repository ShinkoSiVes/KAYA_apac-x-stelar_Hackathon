# KAYA — Demo / Pitch Video Script

A tight script for the short video many hackathons request at the idea stage. Two cuts:
- **90-second product demo** (primary)
- **60-second pitch-only** (if there's a hard 1-minute cap)

Tips: speak in a Taglish, friendly tone. Show a phone screen (real or mockup/Figma). Keep on-screen captions for key terms (Soroban, SEP-24, path payment, fee-bump). Music: light, upbeat.

---

## 90-second product demo

| Time | On screen | Voiceover |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:08 | KAYA logo → tagline "Kaya mo 'to." Then a young person staring at a food-delivery checkout. | "Every day, millions of young Filipinos can spend in one tap — but saving? That's the hard part." |
| 0:08–0:18 | App home: balance, weekly budget rings. Caption: "Funded via GCash / Maya — SEP-24 anchor." | "Meet KAYA. You fund it from GCash, Maya, or your bank — and behind the scenes it's a Stellar stablecoin in your wallet." |
| 0:18–0:33 | User taps a ₱250 coffee. KAYA shows a gentle "Hindi pa kaya this week" + a glowing "Save it Instead" button. Tap it. Caption: "Path payment · gasless (fee-bump)." | "When something IS worth it, KAYA says go. Scan any QRPh code and pay instantly — KAYA converts your savings on Stellar's network and even covers the gas. To you, it's just pesos." |

| 1:05–1:20 | Quick montage of the 4 features with track labels; logos of "anchors / yield / wallets" as building blocks. | "KAYA doesn't reinvent the wheel — it composes Stellar's anchors, DeFi pools, path payments, and wallets into one app that actually changes behavior." |
| 1:20–1:30 | Closing card: "Kaya mo 'to." + team + tracks + contact. | "KAYA. Smarter spending, real savings, real credibility — for every young Filipino. Kaya mo 'to." |

## Shot list / assets to prepare
- Phone mockups for: Home/balance, AI flag + "Save it Instead", Vault (lockup timer + yield), QR scan + "Pay Now" success, Profile + KAYA Score.
- Captions for jargon: SEP-24 anchor, Soroban Vault, 48h cooling-off, path payment, fee-bump (gasless), on-chain score.
- Logo sting (open) + closing card (team, tracks, contact, links).
- Optional: a 2-second "building blocks" graphic (anchors · yield pools · wallets · DEX/AMM) to sell composability.

## Recording notes
- Screen-record a clickable Figma prototype if the app isn't built yet — judges accept mockups at idea stage.
- Keep each feature beat under ~15s; pace matters more than completeness.
- End on the tagline every time.
