# KAYA — *"Kaya mo 'to."* · One-Pager

**AI budgeting copilot for Filipino students & young professionals, built on Stellar.**
It intervenes at the moment of temptation — redirecting impulse spending into a yield-earning savings vault, enabling gasless local QR payments, and building an on-chain financial reputation users can borrow against.

*APEC x Stellar Hackathon (SEA) · Tracks: Practical Payments · Meaningful DeFi · Localized Tools · Composability · Team KAYA*

---

### The Problem (local & real)
Young Filipinos spend frictionlessly via GCash/Maya but save nothing, hold no emergency buffer, and have **thin credit files** — so good financial behavior is invisible and unrewarded. Existing apps *track* spending; none change behavior, put idle money to work, **and** turn discipline into creditworthiness.

### The Solution — the KAYA "Money Loop"
**Fund in (fiat→stablecoin) → AI coaches "Kaya afford it?" → Save it (vault) or Pay it (QR) → build on-chain reputation → unlock future credit.**
Every stage runs on an existing Stellar building block (composability first).

---

### Four Features → Four Tracks

| # | Feature | What it does | Stellar building blocks | Track |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **"Save it Instead" Vault** | AI flags an unwise buy → one tap moves the money into a yield vault with a **48h cooling-off lockup** that breaks the impulse cycle | Soroban contract + existing yield pool + local stablecoin | **Meaningful DeFi** |
| 2 | **KAYA Wallet Funding & Cash-Out** | Deposit/withdraw pesos via **GCash, Maya, bank**; receive local stablecoin | **SEP-24 / SEP-6** local anchors (+ SEP-10/12) | **On/Off-Ramps** |
| 3 | **"Kaya Afford It? Pay It!"** | AI green-lights a purchase → scan **QRPh** → pay instantly, **gasless** | **Path payments** + **fee-bump (sponsored fees)** + DEX/AMM | **Practical Payments** |
| 4 | **On-Chain Financial Health Score** | Good habits recorded as attestations; other protocols read it to offer **under-collateralized loans** | **Soroban reputation registry** / attestations | **Identity & Credibility** |

---

### Why It Wins
- **Real, local utility:** solves impulse spending, zero-savings, and thin credit for young Filipinos with real assets and local rails.
- **Composability done right:** plugs into existing **anchors, yield pools, DEX/AMM, wallets** — builds *on* the ecosystem, doesn't reinvent it.
- **Memorable product insight:** DeFi mechanics (a cooling-off lockup) used to change behavior, not just farm yield.
- **Ecosystem multiplier:** the KAYA Score is a primitive *other* Stellar protocols can consume.

### The Ask
Stellar mentorship (Soroban + anchors), intros to **SEA anchor & lending partners**, and a shot at the prize pool to fund a pilot.
